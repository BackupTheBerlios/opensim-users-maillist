Can I just point out that the reason for the strong reactions on both
sides of this discussion is that we all have a deep "attachment" to
opensim. Whether the attachment is because we see business
opportunities, opportunities to explore new uses for a really cool
technology, or because we've spent a considerable amount of time &
energy creating a piece of software that is truly revolutionary...
regardless of where it comes from it is a credit to the OpenSim
community (the developers, those hosting grids, and the users) that it
has created such a passionate following.

--mic
