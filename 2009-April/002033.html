I am currently testing a configuration with 10 sims on 2 servers sharing 1 freeswitch server. Till now it looks ok, but I saw that freeswitch is limited to 30 sessions, where one session seems to be needed for every user visiting my sims. This limit can be changed in the config file /usr/local/freeswitch/conf/autoload_configs/switch.conf.xml (<param name="sessions-per-second" value="30"/>).
