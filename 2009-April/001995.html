Dear all,
I would like to thank you for your valuable feedback, comments, and articles
you have contributed to CyberTech News so far.
Now the time has come to move on, and we have created the Max Ping Magazine
together with Ralf and Simon. We have strong focus on open source virtual
worlds - Opensim and realXtend in particular.

I welcome you to join us in writing and developing the magazine. In doing
that, we are promoting open source virtual world platforms, and activities
around them. If you have a product, or you are developing one, this is a
good opportunity to get global visibility for it.

I have a dream, I want to see the 3D web (or Immersive Internet, or
Hypergrid or whateveryouwanttocallit ;) to become mainstream. I don't want
to spend another ten years waiting, I have already done that since 1996 when
I started to experiment with Virtual Worlds. I know many of you share the
dream.
CyberTech News, and now Max Ping, is our way to speed up the adoption.

Best regards,
Jani Pirkola
founder and editor, Max Ping
http://www.maxping.org
