> I am running Windows 7 x64 and the only problem I found is I may need a
> memory upgrade.  It is running much better since 0.7.3, except one little
> thing.  The new x64 ability allows it to use to much of my RAM.  I am one
> of those people running 2024sq of land.  So far no lag, and only a couple
> of the regions with stuff built.  It I leave it alone running only the one
> instance of opensim.exe it leaves me with 1011MB of my 8GB.  I use Phoenix
> Firestorm when on my grid and that takes up a ton too.  I run a small
> gadget on my desktop that watches my RAM.  With Windows 7 x64 running and
> only those two items I get a warning telling me I have only 500MB free.
>
> I am a bit worried that one I start building on the other regions my
> system will crash out with an out of memory error.  It has done that
> already when I tried to open Firefox (The browser) while both opensim and
> Phoenix Firestorm at the same time.  I am going to start saving for one of
> those new AM3+ bulldozer 8 core, machines with a ASRock mobo that can hold
> 32GB of ram, and hope my computer grows to fit the needs of opensim.  This
> might take me a year or two.
>
> I just wanted to help with my two cents on the new opensim 0.7.3. running
> on Windows 7 x64.  I haven't yet tried uploading any OAR files.  But I have
