I have had problems with the opensim.exe on Vista 64-bit.  I found that
often I would get Wtachdog timeouts in the console, and the script engine
would pause for ages (tens of seconds).  Also saving scripts could become
very very slow, and often time out.  None of these issues occurred using
the 32-bit launcher.

Neil
