That is an ancient script, the first vehicle ever made on OSG/opensims.
There are newer scripts also posted on the forums; but they are still quite
old (just not that old).

Vehicles don't cross borders in opensims, at least, not yet. They may never
on OSG due to the decentralized architecture.

That said, vehicle script crossing has been implemented at avination, and
will eventually reach core (no eta though).

If you want to obtain working boats, look for 'Hiro's Last Resort' on
Littlefield region on OSG -- I have a vendor full sail/motorboats there.
