I don't think there are config settings to disable any of those things (I don't think there is even a setting to 
completely disable mesh, though there are ones to disable upload and disable mesh proxy generation.  None of those are 
on by default).

The usual advice here is to try clearing your 3.28 cache and checking that the textures are *really* there and not, for 
example, cached for some reason on your 1.23 client.
