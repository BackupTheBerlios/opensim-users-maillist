SineWave and DeepThink are organising a full-on profiled load test of OpenSim this Friday, and we've got a US$500 cash prize to the overall winner and a handful of other prizes on offer including some free hosting from DeepThink. The more people we get the better the results, so feel free to tell your friends.

The Sine Wave Pub Quiz

A social gathering in OpenSim

First prize in the quiz - USD $500

Meet other OpenSim users

Friday 10th October 2008

Start time;

5.00 pm GMT

12.00 pm EST

9.00 am PST / SLT

Duration:

40 minutes including prize giving



Free to enter!
