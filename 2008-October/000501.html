Dear Matt:

Go to the inventory window and "create" a new set of pants, shirt, etc. Then wear them. Then edit them. That will work.

On the region question, one has two choices. One is to do a 'terrain fill xx" like terrain fill 21 and set the land to flat, 21m above zero, which is 1 meter above the default water level. The other is to find a terrain file to load into the sim and there are links on opensimulator.org and osgrid.org for how to do that.

Charles
