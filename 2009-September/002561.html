Hi everyone

Just to let you know I have just found the solution to the frozen avi
problem below :D

It turned out that I had two copies of libgdiplus installed on the
vleinternal server, one as root and one as user opensim.

Once I used yum to erase just the root installed copy (i.e yum erase
libgdiplus) and restarted OpenSim the simulator worked just fine.

Clive
