Sometimes it is really hard to figure out if an issue is of a particular 
distribution or of opensim in general.
In case of doubt, and for the diva distro, you can start by reporting it 
here:
http://github.com/diva/diva-distribution/issues

In this particular case, you are not giving enough information to guess. 
Was this during Configure or while running OpenSim? If it's during 
Configure, then it's definitely a distro issue; if it's during OpenSim, 
then it's an opensim issue.

>     The primary reason for having a wiki (vs. a more traditional CMS) is
>     the intrinsic communal ownership of all the content.  Everyone owns
>     documentation, not just a select few.  Which means anyone can fix it.
> 
>     For those who discover that a page may be out-of-date, but aren't
>     exactly sure how it should be updated, please at least edit the page
>     and add the following text at the top:
> 
>      {{obsolete}}
> 
>     This will add that page to the "Todo" category [
>     http://opensimulator.org/wiki/Category:Todo ].  Bonus points if you
>     also add a note in the "Discussion" tab mentioning what specifically
>     is out-of-date or needs to be fleshed out.  It'll still take a
>     volunteer to do the actual work, but it makes it much easier to know
>     where attention is needed.
