As an interim solution, try shutting down the sims , clearing out the
regions table in the grid database and bringing them all back up
together.   If the sims were offline for a duration of time, the 2
sims might have 'blocked' access to the 7 sims to save resources after
connections with them failed a number of times.    Additionally, 'old'
and dead regions in the regions table may not cause a problem until
later when the sims try to find neighbors that don't exist.
