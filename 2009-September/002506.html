There is a NPC avatar that you can make use of or build from.  It's at
OpenSim.Region.OptionalModules.World.NPC.NPCAvatar

For information on how to make use of it, you can look in
OpenSim.Region.OptionalModules.World.NPC.NPCModule.

There's also some new-ish os scripting commands to make use of the NPC
avatar..   but for something more advanced then a character that just
walks around and talks, you will probably want to build on the
NPCAvatar.
