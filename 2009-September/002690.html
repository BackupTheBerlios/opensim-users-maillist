>> The primary reason for having a wiki (vs. a more traditional CMS) is the
>> intrinsic communal ownership of all the content.  Everyone owns

>> http://opensimulator.org/wiki/Category:Todo ].  Bonus points if you also
>> add a note in the "Discussion" tab mentioning what specifically is
>> out-of-date or needs to be fleshed out.  It'll still take a volunteer to do
>> the actual work, but it makes it much easier to know where attention is
