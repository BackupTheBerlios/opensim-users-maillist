Hi all,

I've been working on a binary packaging of OpenSim targeted for 
hypergrid standalones. The configuration is easy (no editing of .ini 
files), and the updates are also easy (just run the Update tool that 
comes with it).

For people overwhelmed with the configuration process, this may work for 
you. Here's the blog post explaining it:
http://www.metaverseink.com/blog/?p=21
and the link to the download:
http://github.com/diva/diva-distribution/downloads

For issues related to this specific distribution, please use
http://github.com/diva/diva-distribution/issues

Enjoy!
