Well, web pages on a prim are not necessarily a characteristic of the client. 

What we have been doing for the last couple of years is displaying web pages on prims in OpenSim and using an external helping web site for picture formatting such as picidai.net.

There are a series of prims spread around OSGrid and the OSGrid forums have the script source. One of the prims doing web pages on a prim is on Wright Plaza and has been working reliably for over a year.
