Adelle,

That's a great question.
I would hope that OSGrid continues to be the primary test grid for 
OpenSim, and continues to run developers code. Although it's clear that 
you guys who run the OSGrid services are 100% in sync with this, and you 
are super-duper master testers!, it's also clear that many region 
operators and users in OSGrid aren't -- there seems to be a lot of 
frustration when things don't work well, a reaction that is not in sync 
with the concept of being a tester helping squoosh bugs on the way to 
the next release.

It's really up to you OSGrid operators and community to figure out what 
you want OSGrid to be. Maybe it's time that you have a permanent beta 
grid for the real testers ("will the real testers please stand up" :), 
and another grid for everyone else.

Last time I talked about this with Nebadon (several months ago, when 
OpenSim was very unstable and we had the big memory leak crack down), my 
heart beamed with joy with Nebadon's determination in having OSGrid be 
just one grid, and the main test grid. I will beam with joy again if 
this continues.
