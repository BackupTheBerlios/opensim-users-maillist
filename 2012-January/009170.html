There's a floater_tos.xml in the skin/xui folders of every viewer, that  
calls a http request on the SL TOS. The xml is called on in the  
lllogininstance.cpp of the viewer source. So yes, it's only a viewer  
issue, you can replace the tos with any html page and compile your viewer  
so it will bring up your tos upon first login.
