Hi Gokhan,

I never tried to install Freeswitch because it sounded so difficult based
on questions to the listserv.  As an alternative, recently Vivox made voice
available for free to small Opensim grid operators and I just installed it
on FleepGrid with no problems - in fact, it was the easiest installation
I've ever done on Opensim.

To get an account with Vivox, send an email to kamal (at) vivox.com and
request an account for voice.  He will send you a brief agreement to sign
and request some basic info about you and your grid (name, email addy,
etc.), after you return the agreement they will provide you with some
configuration info to paste into your opensim.ini file.  Then just restart
opensim and enable voice on your region, parcel, and in your viewer and
you'll have working voice.

I highly recommend Vivox over Freeswitch since it doesn't require any
additional ports open or additional configuration.
