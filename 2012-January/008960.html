You may add regions in the regions.ini file. 


[region1] 
RegionUUID = UNIQUE UUID 
Location = 10000,10000 
InternalAddress = 0.0.0.0 
InternalPort = 9000 
AllowAlternatePorts = False 
ExternalHostName = YOUR IP ADDRESS 


[region2] 
RegionUUID = UNIQUE UUID 
Location = 10001,10000 
InternalAddress = 0.0.0.0 
InternalPort = 9001 
AllowAlternatePorts = False 
ExternalHostName = YOUR IP ADDRESS 


[region3] 
...... 


An UUID generator is at http://www.famkruithof.net/uuid/uuidgen 


Hope that help.
