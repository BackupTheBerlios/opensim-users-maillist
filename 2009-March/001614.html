> Jani

It happens when simulator is expecting reX style logins
(account at authenticationserver) but user tries to log in with Firstname
Lastname, or the other way around.

The simulation config (opensim.ini) parameter rex_authentication controls
this. When True, reX style logins are expected.

- Lasse / realXtend developer
