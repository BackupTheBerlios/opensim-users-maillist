It's a lot more than that. I suggest you don't use D2 in that case. Use 
the straightforward standalone package that you can get from here:
http://dist.opensimulator.org/
(scroll down, get the latest)
The standard standalone configuration is a sandbox without any inward 
connections other than for the viewers. That's what you want.

D2 is a standalone hypergrided configuration of OpenSim. Different use 
cases are not a good fit and require manual configuration modding that 
is likely to mess things up.
