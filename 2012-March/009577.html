I've tried but failed because qavimator doesn't work on the Lion version of
Mac OS.

But I follow the same spirit by doing the same in DAZ 3D and it works. The
operation is easy: just find the FPS inputbox and change 30 to for example
100. Then you get a slower version the gesture animation.

But it's funny that when exporting to bvh format, if I checked "for
SecondLife Compatibility", the generated bvh will actually cause my SL
Viewer to crash. So I just uncheck the option and everything goes well.
