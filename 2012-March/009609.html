Step by step:

1. Open your inventory.
2. Right-click on a folder
3. Select "New Body Part > New Shape"
4. Right-click the "New Shape" in the inventory and choose "wear"
5. Right-click the "New Shape" in the inventory and choose "edit"
6. Select the gender in the editor and change all other parameters to your  
liking.
7. Save your shape
