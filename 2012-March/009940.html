Yes. This is standard behavior of MAILMAN, the listserv software. Once a
bounce threshold have been exceeded for a user, it proceeds to unsubscribe.
Bounces happen when your mail server refuses incoming mail. It does this
because the administrator has elected a rule that rejects mail from hosts
with no reverse name in the DNS, as part of his anti-spam policy.

Berlios had been notified of the issue and things should clear by now. But
MAILMAN is still processing its backlog. Just submit a new subscription
request.
