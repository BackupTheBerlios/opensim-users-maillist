Personally, I leased a Kentsfield and added RAM to bring it to 8GByte and it is running 8 of my personal regions on OSGrid.  That is the quad-core, so is pretty hot.

The LinuxPlazas (2 regions) and the WindowsPlazas (4 regions) run on two dual-core servers at Cari.

In general, it seems that 1GByte RAM per region is a good plan. That is for a region with reasonable traffic, a few thousand prims and a few hundred scripts. 

Charles
