There are a couple of interesting meeting places and I would like to encourage folks to use them for their projects.

On the second floor of "Wright Plaza" is our meeting room used for "Office Hour". It has a seeting area and two large screens that can be controlled via chat to load an arbitrary web page. This is a good place to use for regularly scheduled meetings. 

At the landing point on "Teravus Plaza" is an out door seating area, sort of like a small greek theatre.

All are encouraged to use these meeting areas (and others on the grid) to support community projects, teaching, education, company presentations and the like.
