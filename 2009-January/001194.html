Heho,

the master avatar ist he owner of the region (what means estate at the
moment, I guess - didn´t try to get a multi-region estate running).


Therefor, he can add estate manager via the viewer.


Estate manager are for now the only way for cooperation (edit other peoples
objects).

Personaly I did never try to change it via the xaml, but use a special
"Governor" vatar to won all stuff.  So in gridmode I put his UUID and name
in, now password.

If I needed to change it, I did that directly in the database table.
