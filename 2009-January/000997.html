I would
like to make a suggestion that various standalones and grids consider putting up a sim on
OSGrid and enable search and add search terms that allow folks to find your standalone or grid.

The region could have prims that describe your vision and even a
HyperGrid link if you wish.

That way, folks on OSGrid can
more easily find the other standalones and grids as our OpenSim part of the Metaverse builds
out with various grids and standalones. 


What
does OSGrid get out of this? Well, we continue to be a nexus for
OpenSim activity and folks can use OSGrid to find other standalones and
grids as 2009 evolves.
