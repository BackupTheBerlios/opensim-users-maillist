This has been an ongoing thread on several fronts for some time. It seems
everyone wants a consistent standard for serializing and package content for
storage out of world and transfer/import.

As best I can tell OAR is still the preferred format.

Please keep us all informed of whatever packaging conventions emerge. I too
have content I would like to make available and would simply rather wait for
such a standard rather than reconverting/flattening the content several
times as the standard emerges.
