Dear Robert:

Normally the two screens respond fine to things like "/10 http://osgrid.org" and "/11 http://opensimulator.org" but right now they are on channels 40 and 41 while Nebadon runs a couple of experiments.

I have not tried yahoo and google, but have tried dozens of other web sites. In general, simple sites work best and flash does not work at all.

But, when I think meetings, I am thinking spreadsheets, simple static web pages, power point presentations, that sort of thing. And in general, I would think our OSGrid meeting room at Wright Plaza is a reasonable demo of how to create and use such ideas.
