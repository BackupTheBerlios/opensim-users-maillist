If you don't want to play with database, opensim files and opensim sources 
and you are not a fan of Diva Distro, there is a way to prepar correctly an 
avatar.

1) Connect your current avatar (assuming you are myFirst myLast with a 
password myPassw) with a viewer; in your inventory prepare a folder under 
the root (eg AllThingsToSurvive) and copy all that is necessary to equip an 
avatar beginner (outfits, Swiss army knife, a shovel, a torch, etc :))
In the OpenSim console, enter the command:
save iar myFirst myLast /AllThingsToSurvive myPassw c:\ 
beginnerequipment.iar

2) Create an avatar according to your preferred method, for example with the 
name TheFirst TheLast and password ThePassw.
Connect it with a viewer and as soon as it is stabilized, enter this command 
in the OpenSim console:
load iar TheFirst TheLast /AllThingsToSurvive ThePassw c:\ 
beginnerequipment.iar

3) When the new folder AllThingsToSurvive is created in the inventory of the 
new avatar, you can equip it to your liking.

This way is pure OpenSim.
