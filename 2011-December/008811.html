When you say it doesn't work, you mean you edited the Regions\regions.ini
file and then shutdown and restarted opensim.exe and the region wasn't
there?  Do you get some kind of error?

RegionUUID = a6a2136d-daa1-4f4e-a136-fd26c913ecfa
