By experience, dont use that method; create an INI file 
directly in the folder Regions under bin.
 
Refer to http://opensimulator.org/wiki/Configuration and 
http://opensimulator.org/wiki/Configuring_Regions to 
populate the ini file
 
Give more attention to InternalAddress and 
ExternalHostName and, 
depending on your configuration, 
InternalPort. Take into account 
to unlock access 
of the port(s) for the router and/or the firewall if 
necessary.
