For an intranet configuration (server and viewers on different computer) I use :
InternalAddress = 192.168.0.xx
ExternalHostName = "82.xxx.yy.zzz"

and I noted that configuration worked fine when sever and viewer are in the same computer.

The first time you run your region ini file, the console must ask you the RealEstate or that region or the problem is the file is not found.

An other test before to connect to that region: when you see the Region <root> line on the console, type change region Big Fat Region and Enter; if your region is correctly registered you will read as an answer : Region <Big Fat Region> OR type show regions

After if you have not your region in the console, you can inspect the opensim database and the log file.

If the region appears in the console and you have an avatar registered in that session of opensim, try to connect with your viewer; the viewer could connect you to opensim; if not: consider as yu have defined the grid in your viewer (depending on the viewer).

If you can connect your avatar and the connectio stops at the region evel, consider your router, your port...
