Neoclassical Greek sculptures to space ships, you name it: check 
www.turbosquid.com.

If you're interested in professional work, there are some very nice mesh 
sculptures on Turbsquid.com, in the $1 to $300 range (US Dollars), 
including the Elgin Marbles ($5 each).  There are also vases,  3-D 
textures for stone plazas,  tile mosaics, etc.

I put summary pictures of the nicest ones (IMHO) here:
http://myjokaydia.blogspot.com/2011/12/mesh-models-from-turbosquidcom.html

You can go to Turbosquid.com and search "Greek" or "Greek sculpture" to 
see what else there is
http://www.turbosquid.com/

Contact me (before you buy it!) if you find something you want there and 
I'll help you with selecting file formats and the details of importing 
it,  starting with something FREE so we can confirm that you'll be able 
to see it correctly in your grid.
