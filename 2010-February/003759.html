Here is a tidbit for those confused.. you know an MP3 on average is 128
Kilibit. So take 128 and divide by 8.. ( 128,000 / 8 = 16,000 ) so if you
stream music over the net of a 128 Kibit Mp3 you are streaming a steady 16K
at all times..  imagine what your bandwidth would be like if you stream to
more then one source .. 128 per person with only a 512 K limit or heck even
a 2 Meg limit.. then you could go max of 8 people. its all about the
bandwidth guys..  keep this in mind.. and I am only explaining this to those
who didn't know and want to know.

John
