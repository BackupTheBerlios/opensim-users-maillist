I wish these error messages were more descriptive.     Unfortunately,
this means that the region was unable to attach to the grid.   The
gridserver will have more details as to why it was rejected from
connecting to the grid.

There are several reasons why it might do this..    A non-exaustive list:

* there's already a region in that gridspace
* the gridservice password isn't valid
* the gridservice couldn't contact your simulator to ensure that it's
publically available
..    There are many more reasons..    you'll have to check with your
grid provider.
