Animations can move the avatar image independantly of its physics representation. For examples see many dances where the avatar can dance around meters from it's capsule. This is how hugs & kisses work in SL, the animations move the avatars closer together. Granted there is at least one free pair of kiss animations which fail to do this, but I have an ancient pair of hug animations which move the avatars very close together, almost too close.
