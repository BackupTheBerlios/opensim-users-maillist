did you checked out the cache-entry in app_settings/settings.xml ? =>

    <key>CacheLocation</key>
    <map>
      <key>Comment</key>
      <string>Controls the location of the local disk cache</string>
      <key>Persist</key>
      <integer>1</integer>
      <key>Type</key>
      <string>String</string>
      <key>Value</key>
      <string />
    </map>
