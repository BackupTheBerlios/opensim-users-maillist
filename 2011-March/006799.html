Just to add my .02

Most of the schemes depend on a central bank, for example, with OMC you "
need to contact VirWoX to get approval."
It would be a shame that after rescuing the 3D internet from a single
central point of control, OpenSim would turn over the 3D internet's economic
life to a new single point of control.

For that reason, the one peer to peer currency, bitcoin, should be given
some serious consideration.
Are their any developers who would package the bitcoin client as a module or
dll for opensim?
A modular approach resolves the debate for those who hold other opinions;
just pick what currency you can use where you are.. and use the exchanges to
convert currencies as needed...  let the market decide which currency people
settle on.

additional links:
http://bitlex.co.cc/faq
http://firstmetaexchange.com/exchange/quotes
http://www.ige.com/
