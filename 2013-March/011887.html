Thanks Justin and Emperor.. good to know its a problem others have 
seen.  As Justin shows, MOAP is definitely served server direct to 
user like the media URL.

The MOAP URLs all show in the list of media that can "play" in the 
viewer and it gradually fills in from adjacent plots and regions even 
if you never visit them or cross plots.  We have two adjacent regions 
with student project galleries on them next to our main meeting area 
in the corner of one of those regions and we see perhaps 30 to 40 
URLs in the play list.

As Justin said only 5 or so play at once?  Empirically by observation 
I thought I was seeing 5 to 7 at any one time fighting to start up as 
I move my camera around... so Justin might be right at a limit of 5?

Its a real pain as we get a cacophony of youtube video sounds while 
in a seminar on our main meeting space.  You can find all the sources 
with the icon to locate a MOAP generating prim... but to go there and 
turn them all off (even where you do have permissions) took me over 
an hour yesterday!
