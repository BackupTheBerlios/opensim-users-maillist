On 16/02/13 13:38, Ovi Chris Rouly wrote:
> Folks,
>
> can anyone tell me where and how the "sizing" parameters of our avatars are
> stored?  Or, perhaps, are there one or more good write-ups on the subject?

Hi Chris.  If you're referring to nose length, height, etc, then these initially come from the different wearables the 
avatar has worn (shape, hair, pants, shoes etc.).  However, I believe the main storage is in the Avatars tables, in the 
row with the key VisualParams.  You can also see these values (though in a different serialized form) if you save your 
appearance to a notecard using OSSL commands.

There is a bit more information on the wiki page at [1].  This is primarily concerned with NPCs though much of the 
information also applies to any avatar.

[1] http://opensimulator.org/wiki/Appearance_Formats
