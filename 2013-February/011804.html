> Hi Chris.  If you're referring to nose length, height, etc, then these
initially come from the different wearables the
> avatar has worn (shape, hair, pants, shoes etc.).  However, I believe the
main storage is in the Avatars tables, in the
> row with the key VisualParams.  You can also see these values (though in a
different serialized form) if you save your
> appearance to a notecard using OSSL commands.
>
> There is a bit more information on the wiki page at [1].  This is
primarily concerned with NPCs though much of the
> information also applies to any avatar.
>
> [1] http://opensimulator.org/wiki/Appearance_Formats
