I've been using Angstrom for quite awhile. Its archiving has steadily
improved. I have done some minor testing in the past and can confirm it
will not export anything that does not have FULL permissions, so, in that
regard, its safe. It screws up if you try to export more than one linkset
at a time, though. To me another plus is that it is both OpenSim and
AuroraSim compatible.
