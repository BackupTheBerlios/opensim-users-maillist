Hi folks,

In line with previous practice, the time of the OpenSim Developer's meeting
(held every Tuesday at Wright Plaza on osgrid.org) will stay the same in
Europe and the US even though the clocks have now gone back for the end of
summer time.

In other words, the meeting will continue to occur at 11am for the US west
coast, 2pm for the east coast and 7pm in the UK.
