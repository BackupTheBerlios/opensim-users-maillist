The OpenVCE.net virtual worlds assets have now been ported by Clever 
Zebra and the OpenVCE.net team at AIAI in the University of Edinburgh 
from Second Life into Opensim, and are being made available as an OAR 
(Opensim Archive) file.  The materials will also be added into the 
relevant pages in the Opensim wiki and made available in a number of 
grids soon, but you can get a copy now if you wish via:

http://openvce.net/resources/downloads/

Get file "opensim-openvce.oar" from there (right click on the file in 
the above directory in your browser, and select download is the 
easiest way to obtain the materials).

More details of the buildings and other assets available is at 
http://openvce.net/vwassets

Images of the buildings in place in Opensim are at:

http://openvce.net/resources/downloads/opensim-openvce-1.jpg
http://openvce.net/resources/downloads/opensim-openvce-2.jpg
