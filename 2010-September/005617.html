You could check if the estates are connected oki to your regions.

In your database there are two tables to consider: estate_map and
estate_settings

For every region there is a row in the estate_map table. The EstateID in the
estate_map table should match an EstateID in the estate_settings table.

When the two regions have got an EstateID wich cannot be found in the
estate_settings tabel, then correct this.
