I have had this problem as well, in SL, OSGrid and my own standalone.  I  
found I had to go into appearance and manually "lighten" the shade of my  
eyes with the adjustment slider.  For some reason, the avatar eye  
object/layer can lose this setting.  Could never figure out if it's a  
database burp or just gremlins, but that usually fixes it for me.  Hope  
this helps.
