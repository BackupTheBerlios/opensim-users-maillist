no, the logic is this:
OpenSimDefault.ini sets it up
OpenSim.ini supersedes it ( if there's any setting that differs opensim.ini 
settings are the ones used)
Standalone.ini supersedes both (or Grid.ini or Hypergrid.ini, anything on the 
/bin/config folder that is being called by you at the end of opensim.ini)

On diva it goes 

OpenSimDefault.ini sets it up
OpenSim.ini supersedes it
DivaPreferences.ini supercedes both "opensim*.ini"
MyWorld.ini supercedes all
