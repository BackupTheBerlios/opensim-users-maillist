As far as I can tell the notecard is valid; I've also tested with a 
direct clone using osNpcCreate("Test", "NPC", llGetPos(), 
cloners_UUID_here);

On some further fiddling with it, it seems like the latest master is 
having some slight issues with baking; where I can tell that sometimes I 
end up with a completely white avatar after removing entire folder 
contents that contain attachments, skin, shape, etc., or sometimes it 
just won't bake correctly at all when I wear an entire folder (or 
sometimes individual clothing items). I found that I had to force a 
rebake a few times (ctrl alt R) before NPC creation in order to get the 
NPC to not load as a cloud.

I believe you may be right as far as NPC's not supporting multiple 
attachments. That's a bummer as I've been wanting to experiment with 
more and more complex NPCs and attachment scripting :)
