looks like what I ran into once. Once I updated to Mono  2.4.2.3 it has
worked just fine ever since with no scripting errors or sim errors ( accept
my port errors which have nothing to do with Mono ). Some are using newer
versions of mono of which I have not tried. But I do know for a fact that
2.4.2.3 is good.

On another note, with Mono changing and OpenSim changing constantly, it can
get to the point that your going to be running your Grid / Sim with upgrades
to death. So once you find the right combination, stick with it and don't
change until AFTER you experiment on another computer for testing purposes.
Otherwise you will end up scrapping the whole project and starting over.
That was our Grids problem.
