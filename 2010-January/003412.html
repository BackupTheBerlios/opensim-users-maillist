Sounds like a start,

I can't make it this time to the UK, but if someone is interested in
a regularly meetup in the Rhein-Main Area (particularly Frankfurt),
it would be my pleasure to gather here (Frankfurt/Germany).

A vibrant community is what we need to pursue
and advance the possibilites for virtual world and application developers
which OpenSim / Second Life technology offers.

It is also important to share
experience and knowledge how other developers are using or plan to use
OpenSimulator.
For instance, we plan to use OpenSim with Android (OS) on a Touch Tablet in
the near future.

If anyone is interested in a meetup here in Frankfurt, just contact me.

Best Regards,
