A technique we have found useful is a seriously large textured prim.  You
can paint grass/hills/whatever  on a prim...  stretch it to the max size set
in the ini  and  "bury it  in the terrain  somewhere above the  artificial
water line....  it will extend off to the horizon very well, and  with
proper texturing  , can look VERY natural.
