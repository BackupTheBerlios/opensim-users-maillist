Dear all,

Hope you have noticed that there are some good assets for download at
http://rexxed.com for realxtend, opensim and Second Life.

Now I wish you could submit your content for everyone else! If you have made
builds, sculpties, meshes, skins, textures or cool worlds for Opensim,
please take some time and use this form:
http://www.rexxed.com/sell-and-distribute-your-content/

In addition to .oar files, you can use Meerkat viewer to save your builds
into local files and then just zip them and they are ready to be sold or
distributed for free. (
http://www.maxping.org/business/news/meerkat-may-impact-second-inventory-market-share.aspx
)

If you have made a cool Avatar shape at Second Life or Opensim, you can use
realXtend viewer to save the shape into a file, and submit it to rexxed.com.
(
http://www.maxping.org/technology/platforms/open-simulator/transfer-your-sl-avatar-appearance-to-opensim.aspx
)

Best regards and thanks,
Jani
