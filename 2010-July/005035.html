> 16:00:27 - [CAPS]: Unauthorized CAPS client 

There.
This is one of the new security devices in HG 1.5.
It happens when your TCP Address (where your client is) is different as 
presented to your grid and the other grid. This address must be exactly 
the same throughout a session, to all the grids you visit, in order to 
prevent impersonations.
So you have some nastiness in your network involving the machine where 
the viewer is, not your grid machines.
