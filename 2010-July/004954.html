Could be your database got corrupted when the power went off. When you are
lucky you can repair the database. (use google to find outhow)

When you want to save your work you should make a oar backup. (
http://opensimulator.org/wiki/OpenSim_Archives)

it is easy, go into the opensim console and type:

change region <region name>
save oar <oar name>
