As promised, here is the target path to use in you windows shortcut:

 

%windir%\system32\runas.exe /user:DOMAIN\account /savecred "C:\WINDOWS\explorer.exe"

 

if you are using a PC that not part of a domain and is just a pc on a local network you can use:

 

%windir%\system32\runas.exe /user:PCNAME\ACCOUNT /savecred "C:\WINDOWS\explorer.exe"
