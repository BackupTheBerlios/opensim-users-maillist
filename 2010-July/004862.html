I observed the same. It appears as if "My Suitcase" works like the "OpenSim Library" folder with the exception that items can be added to "My Suitcase" when surfing the HyperGrid. But you cannot remove anything from them by means of the viewer.

Also, items directly worn or attached from "My Suitcase" are seemingly copied into "My Inventory". But after a re-log of the viewer, those items are gone from "My Inventory" although you are still wearing them. You must explicitly take a copy of the item in "My Suitcase" to make it persist in "My Inventory".

It would be more convenient if wearing or attaching an item from "My Suitcase" made this copy automatically and transparently. I don't know if this is the same behavior as with "OpenSim Library"; due to a lack of wearable or attachable items in the standard library I have never been confronted with this before.
