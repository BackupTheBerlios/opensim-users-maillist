Hi folks.  Just fyi, I finally got time to write a blog post today about OpenSim's modSendCommand() function.  This is a 
mechanism for communicating data between in-world scripts and a back-end region module without having to patch OpenSim 
with new script functions.  It's been around since OpenSim 0.6.9.

Blog and wiki information is here

http://justincc.org/blog/2010/07/16/passing-data-between-a-script-and-a-region-module-with-modsendcommand/
