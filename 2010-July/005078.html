> Hi folks.  As part of my latent mascohism ;). I spent some time today
> updating the OpenSimulator configuration page
>
> http://opensimulator.org/wiki/Configuration

> services (in pure default configuration).

> or put something on the discussion tab of that page.

> each.  The source files for the diagrams are also linked there.
