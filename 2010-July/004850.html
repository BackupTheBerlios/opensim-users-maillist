Hello back.  I have had this same problem (though somewhat rarely) with  
various builds of OpenSim from 0.6.8 on.  My personal opinion, through  
some experimentation, is that the database is either falling out of sync  
with OS or it becomes overloaded and simply stalls or the SQL socket goes  
deaf...  in any case, iy usually happens after some extended heave prim  
operations (i.e. mass deleting, shift-copying large groupings or even  
manipulating prims in linksets).  On a few occasions, letting the server  
sit idle for 10-20 mins did the trick, but more times than not I've had to  
restart OS like you mentioned.

I don't know what your hardware specs are, but I can attest to the fact  
that the problem (for me) occurred mostly on my single processor Pentium  
box and almost never on my Dual Core laptop which was obviously taking  
full advantage of asynchronous packet handling and whatnot.  I hope any of  
this info was helpful in some small way.

Jason
