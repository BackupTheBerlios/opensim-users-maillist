  When trying to upgrade my 0.6.9 standalone region to 0.7 I hit a problem.
 My 0.6.9 was a straight forward single region stand-alone install on Vista
64-bit, just modified config to use MySQL.  Just two users one of which was
the 'master user' (estate manager). To upgrade I modified opensim.ini and
