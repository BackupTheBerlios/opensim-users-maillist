As far as I can see, no log is attached.

I recommend that you try setting up a standalone and seeing if you have trouble crossing between regions on that.

Trying to cross between regions hosted on different machines is, of course, a somewhat different matter.  Success will 
depend heavily on the network connection quality of the two hosts (and the client).

Yes, I think there are bugs in this area.

> 4) What is the difference between a Region and an Estate? I'm seeing
> both being thrown around almost as if they mean the same thing.

afaik, an estate can consist of many regions.  Each region can belong only to one estate.
