I got the temporary fix for the redirect and the port 80.

I used server2go ( http://www.server2go-web.de/download/download.html ).  It
is donation ware but free to use.  All I did was configure the server2go app
to run on port 80 (default is 4001), and created a index.html to point to
http://sb-grid.com:8002/wifi/ from http://sb-grid.com

I know that apache has a portable app but since I am not a developer it
makes it a little difficult to understand when I have to compile something.
Maybe someone out there wants to tackle that since it is open source.
