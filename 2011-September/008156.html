IMO, anyone looking to do RL monetary transactions on top of OpenSIM
when its clearly declared by its developers as "Alpha Software" is
simply asking for trouble.  Unless your planning on a fork and
significant work at hardening it (as the Inworldz folks are doing) its
just simply not ready for that.   Notice also there is *no* support in
core for this and there has been discussion as to the reason why.

If you do proceed then I'd say Karen's advice is pretty mandatory to
protect yourself.
