On 06/09/11 18:18, cmableson wrote:
> When an OAR is imported, do the UUIDs for assets and objects stay the same or
> are they changed when an OAR is imported to a region on the *same* grid and
> to a region on a *different* grid? It is my understanding they change only
> when imported to a new grid.

The asset UUIDs remain the same on import to both same and different grids.  The object UUIDs always change no matter 
whether you are loading to the same or different.
