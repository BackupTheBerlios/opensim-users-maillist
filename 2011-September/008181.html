When you accept an offer to TP from an avatar in the same sim where you
are, your  viewer freezes on the black screen indefinitely. I already
opened a mantis on 13th, since then, I continued to test this issue in
other grids with revisions in the range from r16506 to now and always this
problem is present.
Strange I have not received confirmations about this issue, people in
Craft-World is complaining about it so I think it must be known in other
grids too. We tested in OSGrid in LBSA Plaza on 13th and the bug was
present.

http://opensimulator.org/mantis/view.php?id=5690
