I'm not sure I completely understand the question. Still: Textures will be
mapped onto the faces of a prim. How many faces it has depe ds on the type
of prim. By default, the texture will be stretched to cover the whole face,
yoe can set repeats and offset so only part of it covers the face, or so
that multiples of it do. Planar mapping is a different kind of mapping,
which will, unlike the default mapping, not be affected by prim twists and
taper.
