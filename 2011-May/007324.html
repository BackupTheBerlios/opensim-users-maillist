You will notice that 'computer' tab in the textures dialog of Imprudence 1.4
says 'select textures to preview' or something similar.  It is exactly that
- a local preview, not only is it not persisted, it isn't uploaded or
distributed at all, so others won't see it.  It is a new feature of
Imprudence 1.4, for previewing quickly, which I can see being especially
useful in Second Life where texture uploads cost money.
