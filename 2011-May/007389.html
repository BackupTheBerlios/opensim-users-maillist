Hi there,

have you started mySQL? On OSX you'll need to start it first every time  
your computer starts up. You can do that through system preferences, if  
you installed the mySQL preference pane, or via terminal:

shell> cd /usr/local/mysql
shell> sudo ./bin/mysqld_safe
(Enter your password, if necessary)
(Press Control-Z)
shell> bg
(Press Control-D or enter "exit" to exit the shell)
