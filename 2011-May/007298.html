Using the latest SimonaStick/Diva release with 7.1 I have found that
after crossing sim boundaries, for example from region1 to region2, my
coordinates at the top of the screen (Imprudence viewer) read
something like 'region1 174, 353, 27'. It never shows me as being on
any of the other 3 regions, always only on region1 with the
appropriate coordinates well above 256. Also when rezzing objects,
their position coordinates in the build window have the same
larger-than-256 issue.

I haven't tested this in another viewer, but three times I backed all
the way out, shutting down client, server, database then rebooting
them. Even when logging back in directly to one of the non-primary
regions the problem persists.

Didn't see this in a quick shuffle through known bugs. The only thing
I've changed so far are the region names in RegionConfig.ini, but I
don't imagine that could be at all related!
