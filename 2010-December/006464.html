In response to recent confusion and discussion regarding non-technical, new
user posts, a new user support group has been created.

Here is the Introduction:

Welcome to the brave new worlds!
New ones are evolving daily.
If we are going to be perpetual newbies, we have a support group.
Many patiently mentored me in my early Second Life days. (Some still do)
The goal of this Group is to extend that supportive custom on to new
frontiers.
Please join in and make others happy and comfortable to be part of another
supportive community.

I have sent invitations to those I feel would be interested or positive
forces.  If you were missed, and wish for more email, please visit:
http://groups.google.com/group/virtual-world-educators
should get you there.
I assume there is a button to Join, since I am already a member.  :-)

Thank you for the use of this group and assistance I have already received.

Happy Holidays
