Thanks Chris.  I would like to make it clear that I actually think discussion of applications of OpenSim in areas such 
as education is fine on the -users list right now.  If the volume gets too much then another list could be split off.

I'm wary of splitting up lists before demand is demonstrated by actual discussion.  But as Diva said, this certainly 
doesn't stop people creating their own lists or forums.

As always, this kind of community evolution is complex and I find that the best balance only becomes clear over time.

Where I think we do need to draw the line is at topics which are only distantly related to actually working with the 
OpenSim project, for the reasons previously stated.
