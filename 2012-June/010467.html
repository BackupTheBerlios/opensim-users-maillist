Hello. :)

The default prim size for a non-physical prim is 256 meters, and 10 
meters for a physical prim. There are settings to change these in 
OpenSim.ini. Look in the [Startup] section for the options 
NonPhysicalPrimMax and PhysicalPrimMax. If these settings are fine and 
you still cannot exceed 64 meters, it may be the viewer you're using. 
Second Life enforces their prim size limit on both the server and client 
sides, and so a TPV must patch for this.

Marcus
