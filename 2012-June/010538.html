This is one of the lot of bugs of Megaregion.

Ask the users of your grid to use the *<Type region name>* in drop down 
box *Start Location* in the viewer; here they have to type the name of 
the Megaregion root. For next logins, they can use *My Last Location*.

For teleports, never use region names other than the Megaregion root; 
one in two cases gives an unintended result.
