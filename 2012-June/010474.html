At 11:00 07/06/2012, opensim-users-request at lists.berlios.de wrote:
>Is there any way to get more information when the loading fails about
>exactly what was wrong and what might possibly fix it?

The viewer log file (for Windows users in e.g. 
C:\Users\<username>\AppData\Roaming\SecondLife\logs) gives some 
details of mesh errors in some cases.

I use the standard LL viewer.. which for me had a good record in the 
last year of so, even with very complex meshes until recently.  See 
this LL JIRA entry for some problems in calculating weights and 
upload costs in recent versions.

https://jira.secondlife.com/browse/SH-3055

After seeing a suggestion in the discussiom forums, I went back to 
the older version 3.2.4 for uploads to OpenSim and that worked again 
fine. I could then  switch back to the most recent 3.2.2 and the 
meshes were able to be rezzed and vieweed fine.  It was just uploads 
that had errors at the point where costs were being calculated.
