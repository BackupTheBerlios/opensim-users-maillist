Listeners are limited by configurable settings in [LL-Functions].  These are now documented at [1], though the best 
thing is to look in OpenSimDefaults.ini and override as appropriate in OpenSim.ini.

I would suggest simply adding a couple of zeroes to these numbers if you are the only person using the region or you can 
trust all users not to abuse listeners.

[1] http://opensimulator.org/wiki/Configuring_Scripting
