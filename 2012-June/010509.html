Marisa, You can handle a HUD image the same as any other one you upload --
be sure its dimensions are a multiple of 64 pixels (i.e., 64, 128, 512,
1024), then use it to texture an object, probably a face of a cube you've
made very thin. Then simply wear that object as a HUD in your chosen
location. I find depending on the size screen I'm using, I might have
better luck starting at the center location, and then moving it around to
wherever is most comfortable by clicking and editing the object while
wearing it, and using the arrows to drag it into place. Good luck!
