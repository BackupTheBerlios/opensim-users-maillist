Dear opensim users,

With help from Paul Fishwick, I wrote a tutorial for getting people 
acquainted with the opensim application development model -- something 
very unique to opensim.
http://opensimulator.org/wiki/Getting_Started_with_Region_Modules

It may not look like it, and it may not be documented anywhere (except 
in the first paragraph of the opensimulator.org site :-), but opensim is 
a framework that can be extended in all sorts of ways for building 
custom virtual worlds with unique characteristics. This "hello world" 
example is only the tip of the iceberg. We hope people will find this 
useful to learn more about opensim and start exploring!

Crista / Diva
