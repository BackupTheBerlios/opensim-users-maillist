One more thing.   While it is important to follow the directions on the 
link below, I found there is a slight discrepancy in them as well (makes 
it tough to know what is right). 

The first couple of stepst that work for me are:
1)  new map
2) Design.inflate (click next)
3) On the next screen x = 512, y = 512  and Horiz. scale = 1  (I wish I 
knew what was best for horz scale) (click next)
4) no changes (click next)
from there its your own preferences.

btw, if it makes a difference I am using L3DT standard V2.6
