> Using libOMV..
>
> I suggest you take a look at testclient.
>
> The biggest thing to take away from that is;
> Log in with the account that you are logging into with the libomv bot.
> But do it with the linden client first.
>
> While logged into the account with the Linden viewer, wear your desired
> appearance.
>
> Log out with the linden client.   Then log-in with your bot and issue the
> appearance command that uses your last appearance.
> client.Appearance.SetPreviousAppearance(true);
>
> This should 'un-cloud' your bot.
