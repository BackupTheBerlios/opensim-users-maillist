Oh yes, I'm familiar with his great work with the HyperGates.  I've
got them set up on my own regions.  Very useful devices, and a great
way to promote random exploration of available jump points.

The only issue I have with HyperGates is that, if you don't rez a
HyperGate on your region, you don't appear in the HyperGate network.

What I'm looking for are public and automatically updated lists that
grid owners have created of all the hypergrid addresses of all the
regions on their grids.
