I suspect that might be a little involved if D2 creates the all the user/inventory entries itself.  Your best bet may be 
to create the account and then look through the tables listed at [1] and change the relevant entries manually, assuming 
you are running standalone or control your own grid service.

When "create user" is executed on OpenSimulator 0.7.4 it allows you to specify a UUID up front (as do the various admin 
methods).  However, I don't think this is the mechanism that D2 uses to create users.

[1] http://opensimulator.org/wiki/Database:Documentation
