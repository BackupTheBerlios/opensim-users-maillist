This is actually the kind of thing I was imagining we would do. First of 
all divide the grids up by historical eras, but within an era there 
would still be a lot of jumble, with different stories happening in the 
same spot at different times with the same buildings but people wearing 
different kinds of clothes and so on. I envisioned that you should be 
able to work out what date you want to visit, and only load the stories 
that are current at that date, with "permanent" structures always 
loading. I'm glad you suggested that independently of me bringing it up :)
