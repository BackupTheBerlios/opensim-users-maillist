Go ahead and set those to localhost anyway. That works for me. I am running
Win7 64bit on both of my computers. From what I understand, Descendents2
and Inventory2 was introduced somewhere between V2 and V3. Setting those to
localhost resolved my own Inventory issues. In fact, every Cap_ that refers
to Inventory I've set to localhost without any problem. I may get bold
enough to set all the Cap_ to localhost, just to see what happens :-D
Anyway, with all four of those set to localhost I see all textures and all
mesh objects, my av and inventory loads without problems, other than with
Kokua.

Btw, the only viewer I ban is Emerald.
