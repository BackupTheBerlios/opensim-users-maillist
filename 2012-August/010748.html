IN OpenSimulator 0.7.3.1, FetchInventory2 and FetchInventoryDescendents2 were implemented but with bugs, meaning they 
would only partially work.

In OpenSimulator 0.7.4-rc1, these two CAPs are now implemented properly (AFAIK) and seem to work just fine with viewer 2/3.

With large inventories, the HTTP transfer appears to work a lot better.  Large numbers of UDP inventory requests 
generate a lot of data, and at least on OpenSimulator were able to crowd out other UDP packets on the delivery stack, 
resulting in a degraded connection experience.  This is why these CAPs are enabled by default on 0.7.4-rc1.
