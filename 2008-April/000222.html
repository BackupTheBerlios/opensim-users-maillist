Hi folks,

Just FYI, I've established an OpenSim 'related software' page on the wiki. 

http://opensimulator.org/wiki/Related_Software

The aim of this page is really to hold references to reimplementations 
of OpenSim services (e.g. a reimplementation of the inventory service) 
and links to region/application modules which can be activated on top of 
the OpenSim core.

The only thing there at the moment is a link to Lulurun's Perl UGAI 
running under Apache, but hopefully a list of modules and components 
will get fleshed out as OpenSim matures.

Please feel free to add to it.
