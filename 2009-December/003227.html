The standard URL for the OpenVCE assets for Opensim now contains an 
OAR that may be a bit more reasonable as a starting position, with 
just one quarter section of the amphitheatre suitable for a single 
sim.  It also has a layout that should be a reasonable starting 
position to build a collaboration sim.  It is based on the current 
layout of the VCE region in Second Life already used by the 
OpenVCE.net community. URL, as before for this OAR is:

http://openvce.net/resources/downloads/opensim-openvce.oar

The previous "full" asset set, including the 4 sim corner 
amphitheatre is still available at:

http://openvce.net/resources/downloads/opensim-openvce-full.oar
