> I am working on an archiving facility which extends load/save-xml2 to 
> save and restore assets as well as prim data.  However, like 
> load/save-xml2 this is oriented towards use at the region console, so 
> the chief uses will probably be for backup and content transfer between 
> disconnected opensim deployments/applications.  Naturally, this is not 
> applicable to SL <-> OpenSim transfers.
> 

The software is useless in my opinion. It's an attempt on a legal
or (per terms LL service) way of providing a way to move your objects
from SL to your own OS grid. The software is legally infringing on
those that assume that your work is of your own. Errors, issues with
copy, issues with transport, issues with complex prim situations are
ignored on reproduction. Linden takes the stance that they own the
rights to the objects and that copybot is illegal. Second Backup is
not an alternative to addressing the legal issue of allowing a person
or company access rights to their own data.

There is no right way around Linden allowing a person to have their
own content out of the database and Second Backup is garbage.
