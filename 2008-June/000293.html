I am on the steering committee of the ACM/IEEE SigARCH conference on
high performance computing, networking, storage and analytics, also
known as SC <http://sc08.supercomp.org/?pg=about.html> . I am interested
in creating a persistent virtual world for this community to use in
education, presentation, scientific collaboration and visualization.

I am looking for members of this community who are interested in working
with us to establish this presence and develop the facilities we need to
enable new modes of work and collaboration.
