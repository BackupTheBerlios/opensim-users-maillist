I can confirm. Only failed on scripted items. It replaces scripts with 
placeholders of the same name.

Second Inventory to transfer my shape, skin, eyes, hair and pants, shirt 
and shoes.

The trick when transferring items with textures is to use Advanced 
Restore and have it create a unique UUID for the textures.
