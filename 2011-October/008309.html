Hi everyone,

I am preparing an informal Get-together in Frankfurt am Main, January the
14th & 15th 2012.
One topic/stream/track will be OpenSimulator related, another one is about
3D Printer (open source hardware) and last but not least, one 'track' will
be about Arduino.

The Get-together is called "Januar-Convention".
The convention will mainly be in german language, OpenSimulator talks can be
in english - if preferred.

1st Day is at the 'Saalbau Gutleut':
http://www.saalbau.com/die-frankfurter-saalbauten-raumangebot/saalbau-gutleut.html
2nd Day is at the 'Museum für Kommunikation Frankfurt'

14. January 2012 - Saalbau Gutleut
OpenSimulator, Arduino, Content Management Systeme, Podcasting Workshop
http://tgib.co.uk/ (Vanish) already confirmed participation.
