I also find it helps if you go into the Advanced menu up top -> 
Rendering -> Info Displays and enable BBoxes and Points. This will show 
the bounding box  and points of the invisible prims (as well as every 
thing else) and you'll see a single tiny white dot (point) in the middle 
of each one. This will make those pesky bridges much easier to see and 
locate especially if there are numerous ones in the general vicinity. If 
there are just a few out though and they are hard to find, you can also 
use the Area Search option found in the Phoenix section of the top menu 
and search for "Object" in the Name search string section.

Hope this helps!
