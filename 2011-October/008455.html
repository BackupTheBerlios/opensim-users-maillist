As DutchGlory wrote, it may be possible to filter out the XEngine debug messages from the log.  Or you could raise the 
general log level to info if you don't mind missing the other messages.

The log messages will have a negligible impact on script loading times - this really does take a long time even if the 
DLL is already compiled.
