I figured it out.  There is a line missing from the example file.  I 
changed the entry to read (addition is *bolded)*

    [GridService]
          StorageProvider = "Diva.Data.MySQL.dll"
    *LocalServiceModule = "OpenSim.Services.GridService.dll:GridService"*

and Robust with wifi is working.

Kurt
