As Brianna notes, you need the latest opensim and either the >3.0 SL
viewer or
something compatible (nightly mesh viewer release). There are some
quirks, though.
Basic mesh and texture seem to work fine, but I have found difficulty in
getting
armatures working for Autodesk's evolver product - it is likely a
problem in the
specific hierarchy layout required by the SL viewer. To upload a mesh,
launch
the viewer and Build->Upload->Model.
