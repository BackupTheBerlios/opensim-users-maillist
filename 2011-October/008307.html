> Hi folks.  Opensimulator 0.7.2 is now released.  You can download at [1]
> and see the release notes at [2].
>
> On the whole, the additions since opensim-0.7.2-rc2 are minimal.  The one
> major change is the inclusion of Snoopy's fix
> for HUDs appearing on other player's screens (commit c2586b0 in
> 0.7.2-post-fixes, commit 2b2580e in master).  I judged
> this sufficiently worthwhile to take the risk.
>
> The git branch 0.7.2-post-fixes may receive additional ports of fixes from
> master in the future.  If these are major and
> sufficient developer motivation exists, there may be another point release
> on this branch.
>
> Many, many thanks to everybody who contributed to this release!
>
> [1] http://opensimulator.org/wiki/Download
