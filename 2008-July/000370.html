After a long work, I am pleased to announce you the soon-to-be-released 
OpenSim Launcher 0.2 with opensim:// protocol handler.
Teleporting into a given region of a given grid will be as simple as 
clicking on this kind of link :

opensim://grid.newworldgrid.com:8002/Welcome/128/128

Please, feel free to send me your feature requests and I will be happy 
to add them. More grids and viewers support to come.
