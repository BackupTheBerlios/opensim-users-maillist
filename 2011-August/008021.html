I know this is too simple, but I have seen people mess this up.  Are you 
using DYNdns correctly?  I mean, not only get an account thare and start 
up you new DNS , but are you using the DYNdns updater on your machine.  
If your using the updater, is it showing the green checkbox, in the 
lower, left hand, corner that says,  "status: GOOD"?

Also, on the right pane there should be a checked box, next to your IP 
name you have chosen, for your access to your machine.  I know, it might 
be simple, but I have seen many problems start right there.  A friend 
once neded to use DYNdns and didn't realize they also needed the 
updater.  It worked the first time, then their IP changed and it stopped 
working.  When I told them they needed the updater they found it hard to 
find.
