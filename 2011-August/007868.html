Hi folks.  I'd just like to announce that as of last Friday, we would like
to welcome Nebadon Izumi and Snoopy Pfeffer 
to the OpenSim core dev team.

As I'm sure many of you know, Nebadon has been with the project for a very
long time (longer than I have!).  Although he 
doesn't code much himself, he does fantastic work in testing and
co-ordinating work on the project, not least as 
president of osgrid.org (which provides invaluable feedback by running
cutting edge OpenSim code).

Snoopy has also been around for many years, and over that time has
contributed valuable fixes and features to OpenSim, 
such as client filtering and estate/land management.

We're very pleased to welcome both these guys as direct committers.
