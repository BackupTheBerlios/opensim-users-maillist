Not sure whether the group will go anywhere, but we can try.

I have created a Google group called "opensimbot". Messages can
be sent to opensimbot at googlegroups.com and request to join
(do this first) can be by sending an email to:

    opensimbot+subscribe at googlegroups.com

Unsubscribing can be done by logging into your Google page where
your groups are listed. The group is not public (i.e., restricted) so we
don't
have to worry about  spam, which ends up plaguing public groups.
