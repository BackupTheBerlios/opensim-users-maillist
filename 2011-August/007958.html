For an overview, see here:  
http://opensimulator.org/wiki/Configuration#Standalone_vs._Grid

Basically, a standalone does not allow others to connect their region to  
your simulator, while a grid does. This has nothing to do with people  
visiting it.

I once made a small glossary on OpenSim terms that might be helpful:  
http://opensim-creations.com/2010/07/15/the-road-to-open-simulator-part-i/
