Well without useing any other money moduel of any kind, so I could let 
people click and buy things for $0 I added

economymodule = BetaGridLikeMoneyModule


Near the top of my opensim.ini just below the [Startup].  Now when items 
are for sale and I right click them I can use buy to purchase an item 
for $2.  Probably more if I made it more.

Hope that helps.
