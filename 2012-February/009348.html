> Oh yes... forgot to add... for those having trouble with Singularity as
well. Add in your grid to singularity as usual, apply the settings. Then
close out Singularity. then find the grids_sg2.xml file in
.\SecondLife\user_settings wherever your application data folder may be.
I'm on Win 7 so my grids_sg2.xml file is in
C:\Users\<username>\AppData\Roaming\SecondLife\user_settings
>
> Your newly created grid entry should be at the bottom. It may be named
"the lost continent of hippo" in the gridname section. Change that name to
whatever you want your gridname to be in the grid manager. Then where it
says
>
> <key>loginuri</key>
> <string>http://127.0.0.1:9000/</string>
>
> Change 127.0.0.1 to your IP or FQDN
>
> Save it and reload Singularity. Should be able to pick your renamed grid
and connect to it =)
>
> The first time i did this it reverted my entry back to lost continent and
127.0.0.1 so; you may have to do this one more time to make the info stick
(mileage may vary lol), just be sure Singularity is shut down before making
the changes. If all else fails and it doesn't seem to want to keep the
info, could always make the file read-only.
>
> Hope this helps!
>
>
