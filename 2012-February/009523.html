On viewer 3, the map url for standalones currently needs to be explicitly enabled by changing MapTileURL in 
[LoginService] in bin/config-include/StandaloneCommon.ini.

I don't know why you'd have a problem seeing into adjacent sims if everything is running on a standalone.  This owuld be 
something to Mantis, after first checking with a non-Hypergrid config.
