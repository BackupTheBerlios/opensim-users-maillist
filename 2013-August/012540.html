I’m having a frustrating problem: Wifi 0.7.5 can’t send email.  People can
create accounts, I can search for users, and I can activate them, but Wifi
can’t send mail.  I am using OpenSim 0.7.5 in Grid format with hypergrid
enabled.

I’ve just converted my grid from from diva-r20232.   Before Diva, I was
using OpenSim 7.1.1.1 with Robust.  Both times Wifi was working fine, so
it’s frustrating that there is something here that I’m overlooking.

There is one major difference between my current configuration and my
previous configurations: I’ve now divided my grid into four OpenSim
instances. Each one is an estate with its own OpenSim.ini (just a few lines)
and its own Regions.ini.   I don’t think this is causing the problem, but
I’m mentioning it just in case I’m wrong.

 I’ve tried using two different email SMTP accounts and settings in
Robust.HG.ini:  smtp.gmail.com and my own ISP’s mail.sonic.net but nothing
changes.  I am currently using smtp.gmail.com because that's what's worked
for me in the past.

What am I overlooking?  I have the feeling that it's one of those small,
obvious things that I'll feel like an idiot for overlooking.

The Wifi URL, in case it helps, is http://marin.us.com:8002/wifi .
