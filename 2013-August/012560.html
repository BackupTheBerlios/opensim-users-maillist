change their properties it immediately crashed

Kirsten's S19 latest version viewer updated  recently lets you edit 
them and change a few properties such as the ruler 
attachment/local/reference (indicating it was of type attachment 
rather than a normal in world object) and the 
temporary/locked/physical flags.  I changed them to ruler local 
objects but could not move them.  But I was able to change their type 
to temporary and that made them go away.
