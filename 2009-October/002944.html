At the console type 'create user Firstname Lastname password' (don't include
the quotes!).  Then just hit return to accept the other defaults (unless you
want your AV to login to somewhere other than region 1000,1000).

A list of server commands is here:
http://opensimulator.org/wiki/Server_Commands

And you can type 'help' on the console to get the list.
