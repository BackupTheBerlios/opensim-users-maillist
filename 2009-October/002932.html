Hi everyone

Just to let you know we've eventually solved the problem of waiting
for the region handshake :D

This was eventually apparently resolved by a couple of minor changes
to the ISA server UDP rules and an upgrade of the GNAT server
firmware.

We now have both internal and external access to the simulator working
fine, using the external IP address in each of the ExternalHostName
parameter(s) in Regions.ini :)))

Thanks to everyone who has helped with advice and support on this issue.

Clive
