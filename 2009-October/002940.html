A "grid" (map) exists always, even in standalone. The error you're 
getting seems to be because you are trying to place that region in the 
same coordinates of some other you already have.

If an HG standalone is what you want, you may want to try the distro I 
maintain, the diva distro:
http://github.com/diva/diva-distribution/downloads
The post that describes it:
http://www.metaverseink.com/blog/?p=21

I will be making a new release this week, but old ones are easily 
upgraded by running Upgrade.
