Great stuff Diva, I had completely forgotten about ViewGit.  Both Whitestar and 
I ended up adding these instructions to the downloads page.

> 
> For people who want to get and test bleeding edge revisions of OpenSim, 
> there are two ways of doing this. One is to install and use git, as 
> described here:
> http://opensimulator.org/wiki/Download
> 
> Another, perhaps simpler, way is to download a tar/zip ball. Start here:
> http://opensimulator.org/viewgit/?a=summary&p=opensim
> Scroll down that page and locate the version you want.
> Say, for example, you are interested in 0.6.7-post-fixes. That can be 
> found under "Heads" on that page. Follow that link, and you will get to 
> a page corresponding to that branch, which, in this case is
> http://opensimulator.org/viewgit/?a=shortlog&p=opensim&h=refs/heads/0.6.7-post-fixes. 
> 
> 
> Notice the "Actions" links on the right-most column. One of them is 
> "tar/gz" and another is "zip" -- those are what you want.
> Simply download and untar/unzip the ball.
