Just to be VERY clear, the PORT needs to be unique to the region (suggested 9000, 9001, 900x etc.

AND the grid coordinates need to be unique and "somewhere" close e.g 1000X , 1000Y ; 1001X, 1000Y; 1000X, 1001Y etc.

I THINK my wiki writeup says that, but if it doesn't then please update it to include YOUR understanding of what works!
