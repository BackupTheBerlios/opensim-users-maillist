Login using Second Life client version 1.22.

Check the following points:

    * That voice is allowed for the estate
    * That the land media tab has the private spatial channel option
selected
    * That the voice chat is enabled in your preferences.

The second setting under the land settings is missing from the newer SL
clients, hence the need to use the old version in order to activate the
option.
