Hi Robert,

You need to use the -loginuri option on the command line.  The format  
would be something like:
./secondlife -loginuri http://osgrid.org:8002

to log you onto osgrid, assuming that the 2nd life client is in your  
current directory.  For more information, see: http:// 
opensimulator.org/wiki/Connecting



hope that helps,

brent
