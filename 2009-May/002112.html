Hi all

I have started my blog for Opensim and OSGrid today.

You can already find the following articles at http://snoopypfeffer.wordpress.com/

- OSGrid - Why and How to Join
- Network Connectivity for Opensim
- Installing Mono and MySQL
- Installing and Configuring Opensim
- How to Connect your Region to OSGrid
- Installing Opensim Extensions
- Opensim Service Management
- Viewer for OSGrid and Opensim Worlds
- Where to get Support for Opensim


My goal is, to collect all information pieces that are widely distributed at the moment and to add information that even is not documented yet, like how to use Monit for Opensim service management. I want to provide a step by step guide for people interested in setting up their own Opensim server on a Linux server (I use Ubuntu).

I do not repeat what is documented somewhere else already. I just reference such documents. Instead I try to give an overview how all parts fit together. I describe how to get an Opensim region server up and running, how to integrate it in a grid like OSGrid and how to maintain the service in an efficient way. This blog intends to provide additional information wherever this might be useful.
