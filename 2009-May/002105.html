Has anyone else had problems their avatar not rezzing, and remaining a
cloud? In version 0.6.2.8472, there are no problems, but with the latest
OpenSim SVNs (checked out over the  last 2 days), I get a cloud avatar
that does not rez. This is independent of the viewer used. I've tried the
suggestions on the web without any results:

 * updating the video driver
 * lowering the graphics settings
 * flushing the network cache (via prefs)

A somewhat humorous result is when one uses Kirsten's new S17 viewer
that has shadows--one sees a well formed Ruth shadow even though the
avatar itself is a cloud.
