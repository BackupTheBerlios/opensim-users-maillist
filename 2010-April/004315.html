The User Experience Interest Group (UXIG) [1] is taking a field trip
from its usual home in Second Life, and visiting OSGrid this week and
next week to discuss two topics of potential interest to OpenSim users
and developers:

  * April 15 (today): What OpenSim users and developers want in a viewer.

  * April 22 (next week): How the viewer might evolve if it weren't
    limited by SL compatibility.

Both discussions will be in the Wright Plaza lounge area on OSGrid,
starting at 22:00 UTC (3:00 PM PDT) and running for an hour or so.
Everyone is welcome to attend and participate. I especially encourage
OpenSim regulars from all grids to come join the discussion and share
your thoughts and feedback. :)

I hope to see you there!

- Jacek

P.S. If you can't attend the discussion but would still like to share
your thoughts, feel free to email me personally, or discuss it here on
opensim-users. :)
