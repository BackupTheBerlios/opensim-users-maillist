Ether way should work the same. Normaly 'B' is all you need to set for
gridmode and the commandline option is just another way todo the same thing.
You may be seeing a random problem related to HG.
HG can sometimes be unstable so you may want to test the jump manytimes to
see if it's happing all the time or just sometimes. If it happens all the
time with option B and never with option A you may want to post a a mantis
about it. There should be no differance.
