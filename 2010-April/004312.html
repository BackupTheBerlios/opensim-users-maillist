Hi, You need to also get the OSgroups addon module from the svn forge repo.
But theres a gotcha to that and that is the current svn rev of that has been
updated to work with head rev and will not work at rev 6.9 rc1 or 6.8.  You
will need to find the rev. b4 the big refactor to make groups work. The same
is also true with OSsearch addon as well. I still use the older rev of both
allong with wi-redux frontend with no problems.
You may have done everything right and not have known about the backwards
incmpatabuiluty with the current addons.
