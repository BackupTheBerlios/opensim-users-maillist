Your custom grid list is stored in a file called "grid_info.xml",
located in your "user_settings" directory. The exact location depends
on your operating system. We've got a list of the locations for
Imprudence's "user_settings" directory on our wiki, and I assume Hippo
stores them in a similar location (but more Hippo-ish, or perhaps
Second Life-ish):

  http://imprudenceviewer.org/wiki/Settings_File

You can copy the grid entries directly out of the "grid_info.xml"
file, and into the "default_grids.xml" file; the format of the two
files is the same.

- Jacek
