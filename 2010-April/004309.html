FYI, I just ran OpenSim on my new 64-bit laptop and I had no problems 
whatsoever with the 32bit launcher.

Just remember to copy OpenSim.ini.example to OpenSim.ini AND 
config-include/StandaloneCommon.ini.example to 
config-include/StandaloneCommon.ini

Then it runs out of the box.
