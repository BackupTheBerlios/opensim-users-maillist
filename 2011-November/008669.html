this issue occurs in all V3 viewers that i have tested, including those 
specifically aimed at OpenSim use.  they somehow default to Linden's 
search page, so it's something that probably needs to be changed in the 
viewer code.  otherwise, the search works fine (as far as i have tested) 
in traditional/Snowstorm based viewers (Imprudence, Singularity, Astra, 
Cool VL, Frontier, et al).

also, there are issues surrounding Firestorm and problems it causes with 
OpenSim.  i've noticed this is being address in OpenSim friendly forks 
of the codebase by other developers.  i'm not sure when these will be 
available for use, but i would have to say that the unmodified Firestorm 
viewer is not the best choice at this time.  it is very clearly 
dedicated to SL.  if you want a V3 viewer (other than the official 
Linden package) that respects OpenSim architecture and has many of the 
additional features we have come to expect from TPVs, i highly recommend 
Dolphin Viewer.  you can find it here:

http://dolphinviewer.eregion.de/tag/dolphin-viewer-3/
