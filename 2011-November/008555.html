I got it working fine with opensim 0.7.3dev
-	IM's sent between my grid's users were correctly saved while offline and fetched after login
-	IM's sent from OSgrid users to my grid offline users worked correctly, same way
-	IM's sent from my grid to OSgrid offline users did not work, but I suspect my server config is not in question in this case. Didn't check the preferences of my OSgrid test user account, it may have been simply disabled there.

Didn't have the chance to try to and from another grid than osgrid and mine. I try to minimize the number of my accounts (But's that's another topic ;-)
