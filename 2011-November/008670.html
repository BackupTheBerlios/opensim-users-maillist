most TPV's will support a search item in the part of the gridinfo settings
in your opensim ini's

[GridInfo]

search = http://server.com/search/index.php

I know this works in Hippo and Imprudence. Probably works in any other
that supports grid info

not sure on TPV's based on new version 2/3 based viewer code from sl
