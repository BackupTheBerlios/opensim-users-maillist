This sounds like a great idea. In fact, I'd like to poke around (in my
copious spare time) with attaching to several grids simultaneously. I'm
certain it won't work out of the box... but i think all the parts are in
place to make it work (just register the region with multiple grids & point
the core services to your own asset/inventory services, then treat every
user who arrives in the region as a hypergrid user).

--mic
