I'm not sure about your other questions but the Diva Wifi module offers
both account and inventory management much as you describe and it is
regularly updated and pretty easy to install.  I use it for both FleepGrid
and our campus installation.

Information:  http://opensimulator.org/wiki/Wifi

Download:  https://github.com/diva/d2/downloads

Example:  http://fleepgrid.com/ (Click Create Account)

I believe the module is opensource so you may want to look at the source
code for reference if nothing else.  For others who need account management
tools as grid administrators, I highly recommend it.
