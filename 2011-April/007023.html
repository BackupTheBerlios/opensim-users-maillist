On 11/04/11 20:39, Henry Wills wrote:
> What do I configure to have a larger prim allowance? I've visited a few grids and many have much greater prim capacity
> than what I have (Sim-On-A-Stick Diva Distro), which has a 15k capacity on a region. I've looked through all the .ini
> files and can find nothing.

There's a currently undocumented setting in region config files called MaxPrims which might change this number.  For 
example,

MaxPrims = 20000

However, prim allowances arent' currently enforced - you won't be restricted if you go over the limit (apart from by 
server and client performance, of course).
