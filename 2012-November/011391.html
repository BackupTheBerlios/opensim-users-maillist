The cache reset worked.  I did an operating system-level reset by deleting
files on one system and a reset using the "Clear Cache" button in the
Network section of the Preferences in Singularity.  Each of them worked.

In the process, I realized that all of my previous work with standalone
systems had been on a single system - never over the network.

I appreciate the help.
