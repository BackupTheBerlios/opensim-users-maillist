Unfortunately it sounds like these are viewer related issues with how they
communicate with Opensim, particularly if a sound plays on Phoenix but not
Firestorm, which are both developed by the same group.  Your best bet would
probably be to contact them for support or see if others have reported
these issues on their support forums.

In terms of which viewers have which features, and particularly the mix of
features you need, the best resource I know of for comparing them is
Misfit's Index of Viewers at
http://excelsior-station.wikidot.com/misfit-s-index-of-viewers-miv.  I
don't know that sound issues/bugs will be noted there, but it can at least
show you which viewers have grid managers and MOAP and I guess you'd have
to test from there.

Good luck!
