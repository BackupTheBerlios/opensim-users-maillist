Note about the 3D Internet: it is mostly the same as virtual worlds
technologies with the exception of having a possibility to teleport from
world to world with your own avatar. 3D Internet is like web, but imagine
the web sites being virtual worlds instead. Each world is a service, and
all the services do not use avatars. 3D Internet is predicted to get into
phase of mass adoption by 2015 by Intel and by 2017-2022 by Gartner. Our
estimate is 2018.
