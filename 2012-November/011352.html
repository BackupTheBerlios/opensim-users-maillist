NPCs vanish when the *simulator* is turned off. So all depends if it's 
the simulator or the viewer you mentionned.

A note about *temporary*: this word is already used for prims where it 
indicates that the lifetime of the prim is limited to about 1 minute; 
without imposing anything, it would be preferable to speak of 
persistence / non-persistence of NPCs based on an event (here stopping 
the simulator).
