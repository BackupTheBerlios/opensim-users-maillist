Well, I found the fix I was looking for...

I had originally tried editing 'CombineContiguousRegions' in
bin/OpenSim.ini to '=false' but as noted earlier this caused OpenSim
to crash during startup. I had tried editing the same thing in
bin/config-include/MyWorld.ini, but this had no effect (at least it
didn't crash!). The only place using that edit seemed to work was in
bin/config-include/DivaPreferences.ini.

Maybe I misunderstood, but I thought editing OpenSim.ini or
MyWorld.ini would override the settings in DivaPreferences.ini? Didn't
seem to be the case for this. Oh well, at least it's working for me
now! :)
