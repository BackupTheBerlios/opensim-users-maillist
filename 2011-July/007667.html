These are actually telling you that your Simulator(s) is/are running
really slow.   There's a watcher that keeps track of the various
threads that are going on in OpenSimulator and lets you know when
they're running slow.

It's normal to see them periodically, however, if you see them
constantly, there's something going on in your instance that's bogging
everything else down.   It's tough to figure out what is actually
making things go slow so if you're not familiar with the region
management tools (Estate Tools, parcel tools, etc)  in the viewer then
I suggest going back to a known good backup.

Regards

Teravus
