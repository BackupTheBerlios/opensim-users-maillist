When you export builds out of Second Life with the Imprudence Viewer, they  
will never export the textures, to be compliant with the Second Life Terms  
of Service, which state that you have to be the creator of both the object  
and all of the textures. Any workaround to that will be against the Terms  
of Service and may get you banned.

The latest Imprudence Viewer has loosened this strict rule somewhat in  
that it checks your inventory for the textures on the Object, and if it  
finds them and you are the creator of both the texture and the Object, it  
will export them along with it. If not, the texture will not be saved.
