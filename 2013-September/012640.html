XenaW, I had hypergrid teleporting working reliably for two years, but had
problems started after I switched from 07.1.1 to 0.7.5.  My initial solution
was like yours, to raise OSThreatLevel to VeryHigh.  Out of security
concerns, I did it in just two regions.

However the weakened security made me nervous, so I almost immediately
switched back to setting it to VeryLow and instead I currently use the
following settings:

Allow_osTeleportAgent = PARCEL_OWNER, ESTATE_OWNER, ESTATE_MANAGER
Creators_osTeleportAgent = PARCEL_OWNER, ESTATE_OWNER, ESTATE_MANAGER

I am doing contract work for a company that on my recommendation used these
same values in the company's grid and is not having my problems so I don't
think it's the problem, but I'm interested in hearing what experiences
others have had.
