I'm afraid that that my successful map teleport yesterday was a red herring. 
Basically my grid was working as usual, with teleports into my grid being
impossible for the first several hours after a restart, then becoming
intermittent, and finally working without any problems.  It was probably in
the intermittent phase when I tried it last night.

This morning, after my grid had been running for about 21 hours, I was able
to teleport into it with no problem from both Wright Plaza and Sanctuary. I
then restarted my grid and immediately tried teleporting to it, but all
attempts failed.  One interesting clue is that the same thing happened as
when they failed yesterday: attempting to teleport from Wright Plaza gave
the error message that the destination could not be found, but attempting a
teleport from Sanctuary gave the error message that there was a problem at
the destination.
