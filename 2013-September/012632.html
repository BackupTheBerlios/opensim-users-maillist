I think it is important to remember that LL was not the one who set the
Havok license and requirements for its distribution that was Havok who set
those guidlines that LL was required to abide by, its very unlikely occulus
would put any kind of limitation, generally everything LL develops
themselves are allowed to be distributed such as Windlight, also if you
look at other device support like 3dconnexion SpaceNavigator, there are no
limitations for distribution with that, so its almost certainly sure that
LL will not put any limits on the occulus support either.
