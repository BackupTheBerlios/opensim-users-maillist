As the problem is intermittent, I would strongly suspect a network issue of some kind.  Afaik, there's no OpenSimulator 
config setting that could trigger this kind of behaviour.  I'm not a complete expert on the inner workings of inbound 
Hypergrid connections but I would be very surprised if there's anything that could trigger this kind of thing.
