I spoke with Simudyne folks and the underlying viewer developer at 
OSCC13 and here is some information and links about it in a blog post...

<http://blog.inf.ed.ac.uk/atate/2013/09/08/mobile-simudyne-viewer-for-microsoft-surface-pro/>http://blog.inf.ed.ac.uk/atate/2013/09/08/mobile-simudyne-viewer-for-microsoft-surface-pro/ 


Basically Simudyne have a desktop viewer based on Firestorm and a 
Surface Pro viewer based on a new open source third party viewer 
called Alchemy under development now.

Currently the Simudyne Grid and their development rid are the only 
grids that can be selected and theer is no grid manager.  On the 
suggestion of Simudyne I tried --loginuri but it does not work, as 
the Alchemy viewer is branched off recent V3 code a I understand it.
