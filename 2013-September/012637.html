XenaW, that's really interesting about you being able to teleport from
Sanctuary but not from your grid.  I've been testing teleporting back from
several grids, but not yet Sanctuary.

The problem tends to be intermittent.  It probably just happened to clear up
when you teleported from Sanctuary to my grid.   I tried it just now and was
not able to teleport to my grid from either Wright Plaza or Sanctuary.

The interesting thing is that I get a different error message for the two
grids.  When I try to teleport back from Wright Plaza, I get the message
that the destination cannot be found, but teleporting back from Santuary I

time to look into it today, but will  look at it again this evening or

hours after I first restart my grid.  Then it becomes intermittent, then it
clears up entirely.  This leads me to suspect something in my config files

causes.
