I have used Amazons EC2 service before, at the time I used it It was pretty
unstable for anything I had in mind, I would double check the services
being offered with each tier and also what versions of software is being
run. DreamLand and SimHost may be more cost effective now but it depends on
the needs of your client as well. If they need software thats not offered
by either company then they would need a good developer, if not then those
two service providers should be fine.
