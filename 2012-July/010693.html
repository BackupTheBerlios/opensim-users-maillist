Every viewer have the option to make your own gesture.
One of the ways is to go into your inventory and then go to the menu option
"Create" then choice "New Gesture" After that a new gesture is created 
into your inventory
and the gesyure file is opened for you so you can edit it.

Nameing and place can be different between viewers.
