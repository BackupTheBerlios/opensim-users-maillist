I'm not sure I can help much since I haven't used Hypergrid for a long time.  However, I presume your setup is that both 
of these servers are on the same LAN behind a firewall and you're connecting in with a viewer from a remote network 
(e.g. over the Internet)?

Also a word of caution, I would still say Hypergrid is experimental (Diva, feel free to disagree with me).  I would be 
very very cautious about using it in a 'business' context without understanding the full implications of having 
Hypergrid active in terms of configuration complexity, management and security issues.  Unfortunately, I don't think 
that kind of thing is really outlined anywhere since the protocol/architecture is still evolving.
