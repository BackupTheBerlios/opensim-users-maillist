Hi Chris.  Thanks for the fix confirmation.

The "Dropped..." messages are a little more frequent now on teleport since I tightened the teleport code.  They occur 
when a message is processed from a viewer but the simulator has already removed the connection.  You can ignore them and 
they may eventually get commented out but they're still useful for diagnosing other problems where a client is removed 
from a simulator unexpectedly.
