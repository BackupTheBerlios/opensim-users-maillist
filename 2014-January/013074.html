Hi folks.  Due to a sudden deluge of spam on the opensimulator.org wiki, we've disabled direct account creation and 
asked people to manually request an account on this list.

I know there are various anti-spam approaches (captchas, etc.) but we haven't had great success with them in the past, 
though might try again in the future.

A moderation list also needs someone to go in and weed out all the bots somehow.  So for now, the login page points 
people towards this list.
