At 1:57 PM +0100 6/1/14, Philippe Scoffoni wrote:

>Is there any script or command to purge the table asset of the grid. 
>It reach 58Gb
>on my server... I don't understand why she is so big. We didn't 
>import anything...

She is so big because assets are never deleted. There is no known 
solution except:

- Backup everything (IAR/OAR), drop database and restore. The 
archiver makes a good job at picking only assets in use. You may 
break some scripts if they refer to in-world objects by UUID. HG 
visitors will loose ownership on their objects (that happens to me 
every time a friend zaps his grid).

- Buy disks.

If you want to try a lot of public OARs, boot a "disposable" 
standalone. Some OAR are really huge and will tax your database 
forever.
