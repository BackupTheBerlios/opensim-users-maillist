Have any of you had any experience with the Builder's Buddy script in
OpenSim?  I've noticed many of my Second Life scripts work in OpenSim
without problem but I've not yet tried Builder's Buddy.

For those unfamiliar, Builder's Buddy consists of two scripts - one being
the main one in a primary prim, such as a box and the second script placed
in various objects.comprising parts of a whole structure.  The end result is
being able to rez a box and from that a menu allows rezzing and reassembly
of a whole complex structure.  A form of "pre-packaged" structure.
