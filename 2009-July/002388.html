Considering opensim as one kind of "apache for virtual worlds" (a generic
virtual world server) the solution is a "nanny module".

As with the currency stuff, the parental control, or any censorship thing
anyone could need, at least for me, is far from the objectives of opensim
and, overall, it could be a stupid way to make the main developers lose time
implementing this kind of stuff.

I think opensim is flexible enough (and more in the future) for allowing to
customize or even program the modules anyone could need. The only problem is
that it will require knowledge or money.
