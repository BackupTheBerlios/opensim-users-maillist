On Wed, Jul 1, 2009 at 10:56, Robert Klein<rtkwebman at gmail.com> wrote:
> Anyone else have this problem of scripts not working after upgrading to
> Jaunty from 8.10 or 8.04? In particular I can't get my media screens working
> again and they are using the Freeviewer script.

Do any scripts work at all on your region?

If not, it may be an issue with the compiler binary missing from your
system. You should see something along those lines in the error
messages when your scripts fail to compile.

I can't remember the specifics as I don't have an Ubuntu machine
anymore, but the fix was to simply create a symlink with the name Mono
expects to the actual name of the compiler on your system.
