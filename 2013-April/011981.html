> I haven't dealt with NPCs as of yet, but if this is an issue in NPCs. one
> way to temporarily fix the issue would be to add an invisible root prim,
> onto the object. Then that prim can be on the attachment point, set on
> 0,0,0 for the xyz.  You can move the rest of the prims to tha object to
> where you want it to show.  I tend to do this for hair anyways, just to be
> able to pass it to someone, and make it easy to adjust.  People will know
> it is in the correct rotation, and placement on their avatar using zero in
> both rotation and position.
