RemoteAdmin and the pid file are unconnected things.

In [Startup] in Robust.ini you can specify the PIDFile attribute.

However, there is no RemoteAdmin option for Robust.  You could try the remote RestConsole [1] but I don't have any 
experience with this myself.

[1] http://opensimulator.org/wiki/RestConsole
