OAR file, as said Teravus, are interresting but they can modify your land 
when you load them. For inventory, prefer IAR files ; I found my first 
content searching in Google 'IAR opensim'; and remember "save iar..." in the 
console can save box ful of items, you can reuse them in another grid 
(except SL ... of course)

And for your first question, as a beginner I used help in opensim console

help
displays a list of functions

help {function}
display help for that function

specially for terrains

help terrain
displays functions for terrain

terrain {terrain function} help (note help at the end!)

with that I built a 25 regions grid :)
