That is normal when you're on a grid where there are region registrations
with the grid and the regions are not available.

If you see that message, it means that your region will not try to ask the
mentioned region for map-items again until your simulator is shutdown and
started.   It doesn't mean that the blacklisted region did anything bad.
It does mean that your simulator has tried to contact the blacklisted region
several times and failed.

For mapitems, your region will make a direct contact to other regions
visible on the map when the user opens the map.   As you can imagine, this
takes several minutes to poll them all.     Because the region must wait for
the connection to timeout when the foreign region isn't available, It takes
even longer when there are regions that are not available.   So, to make it
usable, your region will only try to contact the foreign regions a few
times.  Your region counts the number of times that a connection fails and
after a certain amount of failures it skips the unavailable region until the
simulator is shut down and restarted.

Hopefully this helps you understand the message.
