The one issue I would identify as a key one that needs improvement in 
Opensim to allow educators to move their work onto Opensim is to 
ensure we get a really good quality and reasonably scalable voice 
solution.  Vivox does work very well in Second Life.  Freeswitch is 
not a good enough quality replacement  yet in OS I think.  Mumble is 
coming along but we have not yet tested that to see if it meets the 
sort of typical educational collaboration and meetings voice needs.
