>   My main worry is the database.  It has changed substantially I understand between 0.6.9 and 0.7.x.  Should I just bring up the latter pointing to the same database and it will magically be sorted out (if all goes well)?  Or is there an extra step or two to be done?

I clarified and expanded the text that you're referring to.

If you point 0.7[.x.x] at a 0.6.9 database (and earlier) it will upgrade it automatically, though always take a database 
backup first in came something goes wrong, of course.

The harder part is the configuration file changes.  Copying them across from 0.6.9 can result in strange errors - it's 
much better to use the new examples and move any changes you've made manually.
