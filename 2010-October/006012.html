You will have to have to modrex module in opensim. Withouth the modrex
module the meshes are just prims. You can download the Taiga precompiled
from http://github.com/realXtend/taiga/downloads or compile the modrex
module into opensim yourself with
http://github.com/mikkopa/ModreX/tree/v0.1.4#read

You can view the meshes with the naali viewer from realxtend:
http://www.realxtend.org/page.php?pg=news&s=20101013 (there are more viewers
capeable of showing the ogre meshes, like
http://www.sketchworlds.com/download/)
