That's a nice post Klaus, thanks for the overview of some GUI options. I
hadn't heard of some of them.

Personally, I stick with Diva's Wifi web GUI for one simple reason - she
keeps it updated.  I try to keep at least fairly current with the latest
release versions of Opensim and I discovered very quickly that third party
mods that aren't frequently updated are more trouble than they're worth -
at least for someone of my skill level.  If you're capable of modifying
code yourself, maybe some of the other options are more feasible, but for
someone who can't, a module that's perpetually out of sync with the current
version of Opensim is an exercise in frustration and usually futility.  ;)

Diva Wifi info:   http://opensimulator.org/wiki/Wifi
