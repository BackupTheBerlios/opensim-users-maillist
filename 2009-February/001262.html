All,

A good wiki is only as good as the functionality of its search ability. The
search ability is improved by having a good set of categories. Those of you
who are skilled with wikis and are contributing to the OpenSimulator wiki,
please take the time to use categories. It really helps in the long run and
makes it easier to find pertinent information. If you stumble on a page that
does not have a category then take a moment to give it one and before you
know it the wiki will be even more useful than it is now.

Peace,
