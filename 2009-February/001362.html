Just add a "-loginpage http://localhost/welcome.html" to your client
arguments.  Instead of "localhost" you can put the url or ip of your web
server.

For example, mine is:

../secondlife -loginuri http://192.168.2.2:9000 -loginpage
http://192.168.2.3/loginpage.html
