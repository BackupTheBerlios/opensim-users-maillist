As was promised earlier, every week I'll put the Lusitania Terster 
region's file .OAR with all the objects that are placed during the week 
before they are deleted for the region remain clean for another week.
The file will always be placed even if there are no objects placed in 
the region.

All OpenSim community members are urged to bring in region Lusitania 
Tester, objects wishing to be shared by the whole community.
The region Lusitania Tester is in the WorldSimTerra-Grid,  and all are 
invited to use it to make the objects that wish to share with the community.
