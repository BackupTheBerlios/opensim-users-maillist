I love the thing but my biggest complaint - While you can backup a 
folder and all thats in it (minus prims that are inside of prims), you 
cannot bulk restore that folder.  While I've been hearing that this is 
something they are working on, currently the only way to restore in bulk 
is to go through the rather tedious process of right clicking -> 
restoring each individual item one by one.  But for small items items 
such as link sets and boxes of items like animations, sounds, etc. 
(provided there are no prims inside the box) it works great! :)
