Let me be the first very vocal anti-SI person to say yes, it is very worth
it. ;)

In fact, I have a draft of me eating many of my words about how bad SI is.
Fact is, it is a love hate thing, but properly used it saved me hours of
recreating my own content from Second Life in desktop OS sims and eventually
in other internal grids. There simply is no alternative currently and any
attempt would be way behind SI.

Be warned. It has tons of bugs and quirks and if used incorrectly can bring
a grid to its knees. So use caution if you decide to get it.
