Freeswitch continues to eat my remaining life away. After testing all sorts
of configurations I have determined Freeswitch only wants to deal with a
single server and would rather die than accept anything else. The EASIEST
way to have voice available to anyone trying to externally hit your OpenSim
is to have Freeswitch running on the same server as the OpenSim instance. So
to make life easier all the way around get a fairly powerful server and put
OpenSim, MySQL, and Freeswitch on it. ;)

-Robert

btw - this assumes you are wanting to run OpenSim at home and have people
externally connect to it.
