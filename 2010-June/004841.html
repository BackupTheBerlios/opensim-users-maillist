Well I solved one problem but got another.
The Imprudence Developers withdrew the patch
https://jira.secondlife.com/browse/SVC-2941 in Imprudence 1.3.0,
because that solves a problem with prims only partly rezzing an a
Hypergrid jump (http://redmine.imprudenceviewer.org/issues/239)
Imprudence 1.2.2 still had the patch.
So I used imprudence 1.2.2. And that worked! I teleported back over 6K
to my home grid, arrived at a normal spot with normal camera
orientation.
Only on every Hypergrid Teleport I had the partly rezzing problem.
So it seems to me we can be close to a working long range Hypergrid Teleport
