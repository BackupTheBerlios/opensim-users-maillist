The permissions between friends are not implemented yet. Please see 
http://opensimulator.org/wiki/Testing#Friends

If you can change the stuff of your friend, is because you are the owner 
of your grid.
If you need the your friend have acess to other objects, give him or her 
total admin rights, by changing your dbase.

To sell land and objects you have to config your money system.
