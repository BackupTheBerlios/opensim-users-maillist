> Hi there,
>
> In OpenSim r7588, NHibernate and our NHibernate support was upgraded
> (many thanks to mpallari from realXtend for this).
>   This now means that on some Linux distributions, OpenSim may now
> require an extra dependency to resolve the
> System.Data.Oracle interface (see
> http://opensimulator.org/mantis/view.php?id=2734 for more details).
>
> On Ubuntu 8.10 this is
>
> sudo apt-get install libmono-oracle2.0-cil
>
> I don't believe that Windows requires anything extra.
>
> This may depend on the version of mono that you are using.  Mono 2.0.1
> may not require this extra package (feedback on
> this welcome).
>
> More feedback or information on the packages required for other
> distributions would be most welcome on
>
> http://opensimulator.org/wiki/Dependencies
