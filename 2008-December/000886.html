Just a couple of reminders to everyone trying out the hypergrid for the 
Holidays:

1) Make sure you have a home region set on your home grid. If in doubt, 
set it again.

2) Don't place the hyperlinks adjacent to your regions. Leave at least 
one cell in between.

Have fun!
