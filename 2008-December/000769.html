Kirstenlee Cinquetti is right now doing an effort to create a crossplatform

merging and building and seeing were any major problems may lie
[12:14:26] jani.pirkola sanoo: do you mind if I post to opensim-users that
you are doing that work now? They are discussing aboout reviewer and linux
and I would like to point all the resources thay might have to you
[12:16:56] kirstenlee.cinquetti sanoo: sure you can mention it, basically
its the current openlife viewer and taking the realextend trunk and merging

on all 3 platforms with no major issues ( other than branding ) which would
be easy to change
