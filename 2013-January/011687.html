Hi all,

There are opportunities to do research connected to Opensim, Open Virtual Worlds and the Immersive Web.

If you are interested in doing cutting edge research related to the Immersive Web then have a look at the documents linked.

There are opportunities related to all aspects of virtual worlds from studying how they are used, to how they work to designing new architectures.

Education, Human Computer Interaction, Artificial Intelligence, Searching and Navigation, systems integration and Internet Scale distributed applications are all relevant areas related to the Immersive eb

Blurb about research opportunities here
http://blogs.cs.st-andrews.ac.uk/openvirtualworlds/files/2013/01/Immersive-Education-PhD-opportunities3.pdf

and blurb about the University of St Andrews 600th Anniversary PhD studentships of which they are part.
http://blogs.cs.st-andrews.ac.uk/csblog/2012/12/20/600th-anniversary-phd-scholarships/

The deadline for applications is Monday. A research proposal can follow the formal application.
