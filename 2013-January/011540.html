Opensim out of the box supports land sale for $0. This is done by
pointing the -helperuri at the simulator's address and port.

To sell land for actual $ amounts, you need to have a money module
installed and also need to landtool (from wiredux or other sources)
installed, adapted to your system and configured. -helperuri needs
to be pointed to the webserver hosting landtool then.
