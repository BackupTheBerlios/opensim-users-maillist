enauman wrote:
> Can anyone tell me where the gravity settings can be changed now. It used to
> be under ODEPhysics in the Opensim.ini but it seems to be hidden deeper now.
> Not in Divaprefs either.
> Thanks, Erik

Section [ODEPhysicsSettings] is in OpenSiDefaults.ini now. Copy that 
section into your myworld.ini and adjust the settings to your liking. Do 
not change the settings directly in OpenSimDefaults.ini! Those will be 
overwritten when you do an update.
