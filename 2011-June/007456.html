I asked this before, and another person told me that if I find the 
answer to post it here.  As it turned out I found what I was looking for 
in the OpenSimdDefaults.ini.
Be careful, making the capsule to mall will cause flailing and hovering 
when trying to walk or walking up the steps might be impossible.  I am 
hoping that eventually once the ODE is finished one day, we might be 
able to walk a little closer to the ground without  floating so far 
above the ground, yet still walk up the steps of a normal sized build.

I use real life sizes on my grid.  MY AVs are an average height of 4' 9" 
on up to 6'.  My steps are normal for those heights and never come up to 
thier knes like in SL.  Look for this is the ini file:

     ;girth of the avatar.  Adds radius to the height also
     av_capsule_radius = 0.35

     ; changed fom 0.37
