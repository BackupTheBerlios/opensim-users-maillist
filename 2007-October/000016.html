Hi:

If you need only a flat terrain of 64000 m2 and not the 8000 m2 island just
put this line in the startup_commands.txt file under /bin:

*terrain fill 21*

This will level all the terrain to 21 m. I hadn't tested if it maintains
modifications on the terrain (over all because I don't care for the way I
use OpenSim) between restarts.

I'm interested too in a "dummies" manual for terrain modification, or just
in some RAW files to experiment (the ones in the download section of Second
Life are damaged... well, the pages are wrong).
