Surface RT is a ARM processor not Intel x86 it would be pretty much
impossible to get the Linden Lab viewer to run on it without someone
rewriting the viewer from scratch basically.  You would need to get a
Surface Pro or compatible alternative the viewer will only run on Intel/Amd
x86 or x86_64 hardware/operating system.
