> 1. I run my latest OS instance, and it crashes half way through; I can immediately re-start and it will complete, with me being able to use until another intermittent crash is witnessed.

I'm not sure what you mean by this, you will have to be much more detailed.

>
> 2. I had once enhanced the MY.ini in MSQl. Any 411 on what max settings I can use now that I have all of this power?

Unfortunately, there has not been.  I have actually just added a section to the database settings OpenSimulator wiki 
page [1] which at least talks about the max_allowed_packet setting, which can cause problems if set too low (or left at 
the default).  But I've never actually changed this myself so any additions from other people are welcome.

>
> 3. I am running a 100 region flight grid. Would love to volunteer my system ( a sim for who ever helps me) to promote flying, air craft builds, and flight scripting.

The problems you will likely encounter are that 100 regions is a lot of regions to run on one process (splitting up 
across many processes can help).  Also, region crossings are fairly shaky.  This can be avoided with a megaregion but 
that in itself has some trade offs since it's a hack (albeit a clever one) that isn't officially supported by viewers 
(I'm not aware of any TPV work which has changed this - variable regions are something different).

[1] http://opensimulator.org/wiki/Database_Settings#MySQL_3
