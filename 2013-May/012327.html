> This is almost certainly because you're changing it in the serialized
> object asset that is used for inventory storage.
>
> As assets are immutable, changing the data will have no effect - the
> simulator will continue to use old asset on reload.
>
> To make this change, you will need to change the asset UUID and the
> references to that UUID in the inventory item xml.
>
